Clean Temple Guide

A 7-day journey of health, holiness, and discipline — physically and spiritually.

Day 1 — Consecration Begins

Before there’s transformation, there has to be separation. Consecration isn’t about perfection, it’s about posture — being available, being empty, being holy.
Discipline
• Drink 16 oz of water upon waking
• Fast from all social media today
Scripture
“Create in me a clean heart, O G‑D; and renew a right spirit within me.” — Psalm 51:10
Prayer
Father, I’m not asking You to clean my feed. Clean my heart. Remove what’s unlike You. Burn away what’s blocking You.
Journal Prompt
What have I been letting in that G‑D wants to remove?

Day 2 — Fuel & Fire

G‑D doesn’t just want a willing spirit — He wants a strong body too. This is stewardship. This is worship.
Discipline
• Eat only clean, whole foods today
• No sugar, no processed snacks
Scripture
“Whether therefore ye eat, or drink, or whatsoever ye do, do all to the glory of G‑D.” — 1 Corinthians 10:31
Prayer
Let my choices reflect Your glory, G‑D — even in the kitchen. Even in my cravings.
Journal Prompt
What have I been using to fill myself that G‑D never gave me?

Day 3 — Mind Renewal

Pastor Jason always says: “You can’t keep letting trash run through your mind and expect to think holy.” Today, we clean the lens.
Discipline
• Take a walk with no music, no phone
• Fast from negative self-talk
Scripture
“Be not conformed to this world: but be ye transformed by the renewing of your mind...” — Romans 12:2
Prayer
G‑D, wash my thoughts. Let the Word be louder than the world.
Journal Prompt
What thought has been holding me hostage?

Day 4 — Focus & Fire

A distracted life is a defeated life. Pastor Javen said, “What you focus on grows. What you ignore dies.” Choose what lives today.
Discipline
• Write down your top 3 life priorities
• Fast one full meal to seek G‑D intentionally
Scripture
“But seek ye first the kingdom of G‑D, and His righteousness; and all these things shall be added unto you.” — Matthew 6:33
Prayer
Reset my focus, Lord. Show me what matters. Remove what doesn’t.
Journal Prompt
Where has my energy been going that’s not Kingdom?

Day 5 — Clean Spirit

Purity is power. G‑D won’t flow freely through a polluted spirit. Today, clear the channel.
Discipline
• Worship for 15 minutes alone
• Fast from secular music and TV
Scripture
“Blessed are the pure in heart: for they shall see G‑D.” — Matthew 5:8
Prayer
Make me pure again, not perfect — but pure. I want to see You clearer.
Journal Prompt
What spiritual noise have I accepted as normal?

Day 6 — Community & Conviction

The right people don’t just inspire you — they refine you. Who you run with determines how you run.
Discipline
• Call or text someone who sharpens your faith
• Examine faults daily such as gossip, slander, negative thoughts, and focus on chasing Jesus and His purity
Scripture
“Iron sharpeneth iron; so a man sharpeneth the countenance of his friend.” — Proverbs 27:17
Prayer
G‑D, bring the right people into my life and expose the ones You didn’t send.
Journal Prompt
Who am I becoming around the people I’m around?

Day 7 — Strength in Surrender

Pastor Jason said: “You don’t need more motivation, you need more surrender.” You want strength? Let go.
Discipline
• Cold shower and 10 minutes of silence
• Fast from every distraction for one hour
Scripture
“But they that wait upon the LORD shall renew their strength...” — Isaiah 40:31
Prayer
I lay it all down. My agenda. My timeline. My stress. Fill me with Your strength instead.
Journal Prompt
What did this week reveal — and what must change for good?

Covering, Community & Calling

Tsavah

It’s the most powerful youth group I’ve ever been a part of — rooted in worship, boldness, repentance, and the presence of G‑D. We don’t gather just to have fun; we gather to go deep, to grow together, and to carry the fire wherever we go. Whether it’s Friday nights worship at the altar or monthly competitions, Tsavah is where consecration becomes contagious. I’ve been honored to be part of this move of G‑D since the tail end of 2022, and I’ve watched Him work through this ministry in ways that mark people forever. Led by our incredible youth pastor Romar, Tsavah continues to transform and raise up a generation that's not afraid to be set apart.

Through Tsavah Music, our sound reflects our pursuit — raw, hungry, and Holy Spirit led. And every year, Tsavah Storm brings a week of revival, warfare, breakthrough, and marking encounters with G‑D. It’s not just a camp — it’s a proving ground where leaders are born, chains break, and Heaven meets Earth.

Young Adults (YA) IMPACT

It’s a space where G‑D consistently stretches and uses each of us — and I’m honored to play my part in what He’s doing through YA IMPACT.
